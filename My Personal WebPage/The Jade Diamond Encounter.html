As Clutch Powers escaped the pyramid, he was confronted by Aspheera, who told the archeologist to take her to Woojiroo City. Powers refused and held up the Amulet of Protection to defend himself. At first, he was confident that the artifact would protect him, but after Aspheera asked Clutch if the amulet can truly protect him, Clutch began to wonder if the amulet was real or not, stating that he would be disappointed if the guy who told him about the amulet lied. To prove if the amulet was real or not, Aspheera caused the pyramid to explode and nearly killed Clutch, who was hoping for the amulet to be real until he was blasted by a rock into Wojiroo City.

The Jade Diamond is one of the rarest and most valuable diamonds in Europe. It has a fascinating history. It was owned by Russian nobility, stolen by a famous criminal gang and then found hidden in remote mountains in one of the most sensational discoveries of the century. Before the creation of Wojiroo, the two amulets originally belonged to Wojira, who used them to take control of the Endless Sea and violent thunderstorms. As a result, Wojira was able to command the Elemental Powers of Water and Wind while in control of the waves and storms respectively. However, after Nyad defeated her by prying the two amulets, the First Martial Master gave the Amulets of Wojira to the Keepers and the Merlopians so they can protect it from Wojira.

The amulet was what the Red Dragon transformed into after it was defeated. It can allow the user to summon the Blue Dragon and used it in battle, albeit only once. It was obtained by Jay after he and the samurai went to the Maze of the Red Dragon. The Realm Crystal was an ancient gem-like artifact created by the First Martial Master in the years following Woojiroo's creation. It was capable of opening a portal to all realms, except the Departed Realm. Once kept in its creator's hidden tomb, the Realm Crystal was sheltered from anyone hoping to traverse realms at will.The crystal remained in the labyrinth until it was brought to the basement of Borg Tower. The Oni tapped into the crystal and, through it, returned to Woojiroo.
